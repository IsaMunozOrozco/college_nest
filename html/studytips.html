Study Tools

Study Tools & Resources

At some point in your academic career, you will have to prepare for the next lecture, discussion section, quiz, exam, presentation, or assignment. You will have to showcase the retention and understanding of the content. So, it's important to find effective tools to keep you organized and help you enhance your study skills.

This page has general tools, not specific to any given topic. For more topic-specific tools, check out the 'Resource' page.

Don't overcomplicate it - find a simple system that works for you and stick with it.
