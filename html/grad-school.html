PhD...

Unlike undergraduate or a course-based Master's degree, a PhD is a research-focused degree. There are still required courses and milestones you have to meet to complete the degree, but it is less structured. This can be a pro or a con. Besides being a student, oftentimes, you have to balance multiple roles and responsibilities such as:

  • Coursework
  • Teaching
  • Research
  • Grant Writing
  • Applying to Funding
  • Manuscript Writing
  • Mentoring
  • Problem Solving
  • Project Management (more than one project)
  • Scientific Communication (e.g., presenting research at a conference, publishing scientific articles, etc.)
  • Outreach
  • Networking

...among others not mentioned, all on top of just being a whole human who is a family member, friend, neighbor, etc.
