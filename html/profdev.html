Professional Development

Professional development is the ongoing process of acquiring new skills and knowledge to enhance your performance and advance in your career. This can look like taking courses, attending workshops or conferences, earning certificates, and getting support from mentors or networking with others. It's a process that helps with both personal and professional growth.

Most universities, institutions, organizations, associations, conferences, and maybe even your site of work have professional development opportunities, so it's always a great way to start there. Nonetheless, below you will find resources for the different components of professional development.
